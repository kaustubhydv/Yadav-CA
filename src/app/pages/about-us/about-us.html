Executive Committee

Prof. Shashi Kant

Chairman

Prof. Shashi Kant is a professor of forest resource economics and sustainability management at the University of Toronto. He founded and directed the MScSM Program and leads global research through the New Frontiers of Forest Economics think tank. With extensive publications and editorial roles, he’s a recipient of honors like the Order of Ontario and the Queen’s Award for Forestry. He holds degrees from BITS Pilani and the University of Toronto.

Prof. Satyendra Narayan Yadav

President

Prof. Satyendra Narayan Yadav is a professor of applied computing at Sheridan since 1999. He holds a PhD from the University of Waterloo and a master’s from the University of California. With over 35 years of teaching and research in computing, geosciences, and geo-engineering, he has published widely and contributed to national and UNDP-funded projects.

Manju Yadav

VP - Cultural & Family Engagements

Manju is originally from Gurgaon, Haryana, and moved to Canada in 2014 to pursue new professional and personal opportunities. She currently works at TD Insurance, building a successful career in the industry. Since 2016, Manju has been an active member of the Yadav Association of Canada, contributing to community initiatives that promote unity and cultural heritage. She strongly values staying connected to her roots while giving back to the community.

Manish Yadav

VP - Youth Development

Manish Kumar Yadav is a seasoned professional serving as Country Head (Canada) and Vice President of AI & Business Development at Xforia Technologies Inc. A permanent Canadian resident based in Toronto, he holds an MCA (Honors) and an MBA in Human Resources. He is a PhD Scholar in AI & HR.

Kishor Yadav

Treasurer & CFO

Kishor Yadav, CPA (Canada & USA), CGA, ACCA (UK), MBA, LLB, M.Com, B.Com Kishor Yadav is a highly qualified and internationally designated accounting professional with CPA credentials in both Canada and the U.S., as well as CGA and ACCA (UK) certifications. He also holds advanced academic degrees in business, law, and commerce, including an MBA, LLB, M.Com, and B.Com. Currently, Kishor is engaged in the administration of hedge funds, where he applies his expertise in financial operations, regulatory compliance, and global accounting standards.

Hemant Yadav

Joint Secretary

Hemant Yadav is a seasoned IT leader with over 25 years of experience in program management, digital transformation, and financial technology across India and Canada. Since moving to Canada in 2014, he has led major initiatives in top banks, known for aligning tech strategy with business goals. Originally from Kanpur, he actively contributes to the Yadav Canada Association, promoting mentorship, cultural pride, and community empowerment. Hemant blends professional excellence with a strong commitment to service and leadership.

Vardhaman Yadav

General Secretary

Vardhman Yadav is a senior IT professional with a strong academic foundation and over a decade of global experience in the technology sector. Originally from Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, he completed his schooling at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, known for nurturing talent from rural India. He went on to earn a Master’s in Computer Applications (MCA), which laid the groundwork for his successful career in IT. Over the years, Vardhman has lived and worked in various parts of the world, gaining diverse professional and cultural insights, and is currently based in the Toronto area, where he continues to contribute his expertise to the tech industry.
