Welcome to Yadav Association of Canada

Culture & Community, Across Generations

Yadav Association of Canada (YAOC) is a Toronto-based non-profit supporting the Yadav community in Canada. YAOC helps new immigrants through networking and hosts cultural events like Holi and Diwali to foster community connections.

Our Mission

Established in 2009 and based in Toronto, Ontario, the Yadav Association of Canada (YAOC) is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to serving the Yadav community across Canada. Our mission is to support and connect community members—especially new immigrants—through a welcoming network that fosters friendship, cultural celebration, and mutual support.

YAOC regularly organizes social events, cultural festivals like Holi and Diwali, and networking gatherings to help members and their families stay connected and engaged. The association serves as a vibrant platform for building community bonds, sharing experiences, and celebrating our rich heritage together.
